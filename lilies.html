Lilies

There are 27 possible gene combinations for lilies in Animal Crossing New Horizons, but there are only 6 possible colors of lilies. By laying out all of the possible gene combinations in the grid below, we can start to get an idea of which combinations of genes produce which colors.

Click on any two lilies (or the same lily twice) to highlight their possible offspring, then hover your mouse over the offspring to see their probability.
