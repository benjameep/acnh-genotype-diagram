Pansies

There are 27 possible gene combinations for pansies in Animal Crossing New Horizons, but there are only 6 possible colors of pansies. By laying out all of the possible gene combinations in the grid below, we can start to get an idea of which combinations of genes produce which colors.

Click on any two pansies (or the same pansy twice) to highlight their possible offspring, then hover your mouse over the offspring to see their probability.

Try using this chart while following along with Aeter's breeding guide.
