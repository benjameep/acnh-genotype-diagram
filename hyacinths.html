Hyacinths

There are 27 possible gene combinations for hyacinths in Animal Crossing New Horizons, but there are only 7 possible colors of hyacinths. By laying out all of the possible gene combinations in the grid below, we can start to get an idea of which combinations of genes produce which colors.

Click on any two hyacinths (or the same hyacinth twice) to highlight their possible offspring, then hover your mouse over the offspring to see their probability.
